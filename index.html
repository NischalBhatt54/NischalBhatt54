Why Cordyceps

Cordyceps has been traditionally valued across Himalayan cultures for vitality, endurance and resilience. Known historically as a rare alpine fungus, it has long been associated with stamina, breath and sustained energy.

Our Approach

SHILA focuses on cultivating pure fruiting bodies using controlled conditions that preserve the integrity of this remarkable fungus. No fillers, no powders — only the fruiting body.
